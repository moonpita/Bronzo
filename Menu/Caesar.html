Caeser salad

With Baked-Smoked Cashew and Avocado Sauce

Lettuce leaves with avocado and olive oil sauce with rye crisps, cherry tomatoes and baked-smoked cashew nuts Rinse the lettuce leaves, dry on a paper towel, tear, put in a bowl and season with sauce, mix well and put on a plate. Wash the cherry tomatoes, cut into halves and put on top of the seasoned salad. Add baked-smoked cashews and rype chips.

Composition

Letuuce, leaves 150g
Avocado Sauce 90g
Rye Crisps 30g
Cherry Tomatoes 150g
Cashew BRONZO 1pack

AVOCADO SAUCE:
Avocado, peeled 120g
Almond Milk 100g
Dijon Mustard 30g
Soy Sauce 15g
Extra Virgin Olive Oil 15g
Garlic, peeled 10g
Parsley, leaves 5g
sea Salt 2g
Ground Black Pepper 1g

Mix everything with a blender

Rye Crisps
Rye Bread 100g
Garlic, peeled 5g
Vegetable Oil 50g
Dried Paprika 10g

Cut out the bread crust and slice the crumb. Spray with oil. Stir with paprika and garlic, bake at 280 F (Fahrenheit Temperature) for 7 minutes.
