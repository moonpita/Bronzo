Coconut Granola

Coconut Granola with Baked-Smoked Almonds

Soak the almonds overnight. The next day, peel the coconut, cut into thing slices, put on a parchment and bake in an oven heated to 250 F (Fahrenheit temperature) for 40 minutes. Drain the water from the almonds, peel finely chop and put together with the raisins to the coconut, 15 minutes before the coconut is ready. Sprinkle the finished Granola with cinnamon and mix with coconut.

Composition

Fresh Coconut 2 pieces
Coconut Flakes 100g
Raisins 100g
Ground Cinnamon 2g
Almonds BRONZO 1pack
