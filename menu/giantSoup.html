Red Giant Soup

Vegetable soup with Cherry and Baked-Smoked Brazil Nut

Cut cabbage, carrots, baked beet root into strips. Fry carrots, green and sour cabbage in vegetable oil, add baked beet root, tomatoes, topinambur syrup, Brazil nut and cherry, pour water and cook after boiling for 20 minutes over low heat. Salt to taste.

Composition

Purified Weight of All Ingredients
Carrot 30g
Green Cabbage 40g
Sour Cabbage 20g
Baked Beet Root 120g
Tomatoes in their own juice 120g
Sea Salt 50g
Topinambur Syrup 30g
Fresh-Frozen Cherry 50g
