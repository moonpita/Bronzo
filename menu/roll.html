Spring roll

with Guacamole, Quinoa, Spinach, Cucumber and Baked-Smoked Brazil Nut

Mix quinoa with guacamole. Cut cucumbers and carrots into thin strips, chop the Brazil nut. Soak rice paper in cold water, put on it a mixture of quinoa and guacamole, Brazil nut, then cucumber and carrots, sprinkle with black pepper and add chopped spinach leaves. Roll it, tucking the edges inward and allow 30 seconds to stay.


Rice Paper 3 sheets
Guacamole 150g
Boiled Quinoa 150g
Fresh Carrot, peeled 10g
Fresh Cucumber 150g
Fresh Spinach 30g
Mix of Peppers 4g
Brazil nut BRONZO 1pack

Guacamole Sauce:

Avocado, peeled 130g
Juice of a Half a Lime
Garlic, peeled 3g
Fresh Tomatoes 50g
Fresh Coriander 10g
Extra Virgin Olive Oil 15g
Sea Salt 3g
Ground Black Pepper 1g
Topinambur Syrup 8g

Peel avocados and chop it in large pieces. Add tomatoes after removing the seeds and cutting together with the skin into small cubes. Mix with olive oil, grated garlic and finely chopper coriander leaves, squeeze lime juice, add salt, pepper, syrup, and mix with a form until smooth.
