Maple Mousse

Composition


Almond Milk 150g
Vegetable Cream 35g
Bitter Chocolate 70% 60g
Gelatin 4g
Maple Syrup 2g

Mix chocolate, almond milk and maple syrup in a ladle. Warm the ladle in a pan with hot water and mix until smooth. Pour gelatin with cold water.After swelling, remove the gelatin from the water, squeeze and add to the chocolate mass.Cool the chocolate mass to 110 F(Fahrenheit temperature).
Whip vegetable cream. Combine the chocolate mass with cream, then mix.Pour the mass into a pastry bag. Put in the refrigerator for 5 minutes.
