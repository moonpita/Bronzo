Caeser salad

With Baked-Smoked Cashew and Avocado Sauce

Rinse the lettuce leaves, spread them over a paper towel and let them air try. Tear the leaves, put them in a bowl and drizzle with the sauce, toss everything and put on a plate. Wash the cherry tomatoes, cut into halves and put on top of the seasoned salad. Cut the crust off the bread and slice into thick pieces. Spray with oil and season with paprika and garlic, bake at 280 F (Fahrenheit) for 7 minutes. Add Baked smoked cashews and rye chips to the salad.

Ingredients


Lettuce leaves 150g
Avocado Sauce 90g
Rye Crisps 30g
Cherry Tomatoes 150g
Cashew BRONZO 1pack

Avocado sauce


Avocado, peeled 120g
Almond Milk 100g
Dijon Mustard 30g
Soy Sauce 15g
Extra Virgin Olive Oil 15g
Garlic, peeled 10g
Parsley leaves 5g
Sea Salt 2g
Ground Black Pepper 1g

Croutons


Rye Crisps
Rye Bread 100g
Garlic, peeled 5g
Vegetable Oil 50g
Dried Paprika 10g
