Baked-Smoked BRAZIL NUTS

Brazil nuts help to regulate blood sugar levels and reduce bad cholesterol in the body.
Brazil nuts have anti-aging features.A couple of nuts a day makes up for the lack of selenium in the body, which slows down aging. It boosts immune system, and is also a preventive remedy for cancer.
Production Director combines hardwoods of beech, oak and maple to smoke Brazil nuts. He adds a few flakes of cherry and apple trees to add some zest.
