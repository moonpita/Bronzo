Baked-Smoked PISTACHIOS

Pistachios have a beneficial impact on psycho-emotional state as well as tonic and antioxidant systemic effect. They can regulate metabolic processes and eliminate toxins from intestines. Pistachios are best known for their high magnesium and potassium content, which can strengthen the walls of blood vessels and stabilize the heart rhythm.
Lutein in pistachios improves visual acuity and is a nice preventive care that helps to maintain eye health.
Pistachios slow down the natural aging process, improve the tone and elasticity as well as the overall condition of the skin, and make it firmer.
During the smoking process, the Production Director mixes alder, juniper and maple for the brisker taste. He adds apple and pear flakes to harmonize the blend.
