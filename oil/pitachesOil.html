Pistachio oil

Since ancient times pistachio oil has been used for curative purposes, and it became especially popular in the East. Pistachio oil is effective in treating heart diseases and good for digestion. Pistachio oil improves mood, rejuvenates and tones the body.

unsaturated fatty acids 87%:

(Omega-3)

(Omega-6)
oleic acid 53%
(Omega-9)

palmitic acid 11%
stearic acid 2%
vitamins: A, B1, E(33%)
trace elements: folic, acid, amino acids, tannin, minerals

HEALTH-PROMOTING EFFECTS:

-Good antioxidant, rejuvenating body

- Reduces susceptibility to heart diseases

- Useful for people suffering from chronic anemia

- Increases sexual function

- Prevents skin from drying, chapping, cracking

- Helps to strengthen hair and nails
