Brazil nut oil

Brazil nut oil has anti-inflammatory and wound healing properties. It is a powerful antioxidant and fights free radicals that cause aging in the body.
It boosts the processes of rejuvenation, prevents and reduces wrinkles, softens rough skin areas and increases skin elasticity.

INGREDIENTS:

unsaturated fatty acids 74%:
linolenic acid 25%
(Omega-3)
oleic acid 49%
(Omega-9)
saturated fatty acids 26%:
palmitic acid 16%
stearic acid 10%
vitamins: E, A, D
calcium, phosphorus, iron, selenium, magnesium, alpha, beta, delta and gamma tocopherols

HEALTH-PROMOTING EFFECTS:

- Has a powerful antioxidant effect, prevents aging of the body, prevents the prostate cancer

- Slows down the viral diseases in patients with HIV infection

- Helps to lower cholesterol levels and keep your blood sugar levels under control

- Has a positive impact on the nervous system, relieving stress
